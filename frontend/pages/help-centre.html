Frequently Asked Questions

My M-Pesa payment failed — what do I do? +

If your M-Pesa STK push failed, first check: (1) you have sufficient M-Pesa balance, (2) your PIN is correct, (3) you have a valid Safaricom SIM. If the issue persists, the booking will not be confirmed and no money will be deducted. Try again or contact support with your booking reference number.

How long does a refund take? +

Refunds are processed via M-Pesa B2C transfer and typically arrive within:

  • Cancellation before job starts: 2–4 hours
  • Dispute resolved in your favour: 2 business days
  • Auto-release failure: 2 business days

You'll receive an SMS confirmation when the refund is sent.

Can I cancel a booking after paying? +

Yes — you can cancel a booking up to 1 hour before the agreed start time for a full refund. Cancellations within 1 hour of the start time may incur a 20% cancellation fee to compensate the fundi for lost time. Cancellations after the job has started are handled through the dispute process.

Why hasn't my ID verification been approved yet? +

Most ID verifications are completed within 2–4 hours during business hours (Mon–Fri 8am–6pm EAT). If it's been more than 24 hours, the most common reasons are: (1) the ID photo is blurry or partially covered, (2) the selfie doesn't match the ID photo clearly, (3) the ID number entered doesn't match the document. Check your email or SMS for a specific rejection reason and resubmit.

How do I change my phone number? +

Phone number changes require identity verification to prevent fraud. Go to My Profile → Contact & Location → Edit → request phone number change. You'll need to verify both your old and new number with OTP codes. If you've lost access to your old number, contact support with your ID number for manual verification.

A fundi damaged my property — what are my options? +

First, document the damage with photos and timestamps immediately. Then open a dispute within 72 hours of the job via My Hires → raise a dispute, selecting "Property was damaged". KaziX admin will review evidence from both sides. For significant damage, you may also pursue civil remedies — KaziX will provide the fundi's verified details to law enforcement if required.
