Trust and Safety

Privacy Policy

We collect only what we need to run escrow-backed jobs, verify identities, and keep both clients and fundis safe. This policy explains what we gather, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Effective: March 18, 2026 Applies to clients and fundis Kenya operations
Data We Collect
Account details, job activity, M-Pesa escrow events, device signals, and support communications.
How We Use It
To match jobs, secure payments, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, and improve the platform.
Your Control
Access and update your profile, download records, and request deletion where allowed.
Policy Details

What We Collect

We collect the information you provide during onboarding, booking, and dispute resolution. We also capture system events that help us run escrow and protect both parties.

  • Profile data like name, phone number, trade, ID verification, and location.
  • Job activity such as quotes, bookings, milestones, and completion confirmations.
  • Payment signals including escrow holds, releases, and refunds via M-Pesa.
  • Support and dispute messages, uploaded evidence, and resolution notes.

How We Use Information

We use data to operate the marketplace safely and reliably.

  • Verify identities, reduce fraud, and enforce platform standards.
  • Run escrow workflows and issue transaction notifications.
  • Provide dispute resolution and compliance reporting.
  • Improve search ranking and recommendations for clients and fundis.

Sharing and Disclosure

We share only what is required to complete a job, comply with the law, or keep the marketplace safe. We never sell personal data.

  • Clients and fundis see details needed to complete a booking.
  • Payment partners receive escrow instructions and transaction metadata.
  • Regulators or law enforcement may receive records when required.

Retention and Security

We retain records for as long as necessary to provide services, meet legal obligations, and resolve disputes. We use access controls, encryption, and audit logs to protect sensitive data.

Escrow dispute evidence
Dispute attachments and resolution notes are retained to protect both parties and document escrow releases.

Your Choices

You can update your profile, request account deletion, and manage marketing preferences from your profile settings. Some records must be retained for legal or financial reporting.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, reach out via the Help Centre or email our compliance team at privacy@kazix.co.ke.
