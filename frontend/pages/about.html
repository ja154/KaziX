Who We Are

Built to make hiring fundis feel effortless

KaziX connects homeowners and businesses with skilled fundis, backed by escrow payments and a dispute process designed to protect both sides.

Our Mission

Safer local hiring for real work.

We believe great local services should be easy to find and safe to pay for. KaziX was created to remove the stress from hiring by verifying pros, holding payments in escrow, and giving both parties clear expectations.

We partner with trusted payment infrastructure to make sure work is confirmed before funds are released, while keeping the experience fast and familiar for people across Kenya.
