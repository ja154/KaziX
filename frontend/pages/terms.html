Accounts and Identity

You are responsible for keeping your account secure and for providing accurate profile and verification data. We may suspend accounts with false or misleading information.

  • One person or business per account unless approved.
  • Keep phone numbers and M-Pesa details current.
  • Identity checks are required for high value jobs.

Bookings and Escrow

Clients deposit funds into escrow before work begins. Funds are released when milestones are confirmed or after dispute resolution.

Milestone confirmation
If a client does not respond within the confirmation window, KaziX may release payment to the fundi after reviewing available evidence.

Dispute Resolution

Either party can open a dispute within the dispute window shown in the booking details. We review evidence and communication history before releasing escrow funds.

  • Provide clear photos, invoices, or delivery confirmations.
  • Respond within the timeline shared in the dispute console.
  • Admin decisions are logged for compliance and audit.

Pro Conduct

Fundis are expected to arrive on time, complete work to agreed standards, and communicate progress. Abuse or discrimination is not tolerated.

Termination and Liability

We may suspend or terminate accounts that violate these terms. KaziX is not liable for indirect damages but will always follow escrow rules and dispute outcomes.

Changes to Terms

We will notify users of material changes via email or in-product notices. Continued use means acceptance of updated terms.
