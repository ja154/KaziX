FAQ

Common Questions

Is KaziX free to use for clients? +

Yes — posting jobs and contacting fundis is completely free for clients. You only pay the agreed job fee via M-Pesa when you hire a fundi. There are no subscription fees or hidden charges.

How is the 10% fee charged? +

The 10% platform fee is deducted from the fundi's payment — not from the client. If you agree KES 1,000 for a job, you pay KES 1,000 and the fundi receives KES 900. This keeps the service free for clients.

What if the fundi doesn't show up? +

If the fundi doesn't arrive, your escrow is automatically refunded within 2 business days. You can also open a dispute immediately and our admin team will resolve it within 24 hours. Your M-Pesa payment is always safe.

How do I know the fundi is trustworthy? +

Every fundi with the green "Verified" badge has been verified with a Kenya National ID and selfie match using our KYC system. Additionally, reviews on KaziX can only be left by verified clients who actually hired and paid — so ratings are 100% real.

Can I negotiate the price with the fundi? +

Absolutely. Use the in-app messaging to discuss scope and price before committing. Most fundis are flexible, especially for larger jobs. The M-Pesa payment is only triggered when you both agree on a final amount.

How fast will a fundi respond to my job? +

On average, you'll receive your first application within 20 minutes of posting. For urgent jobs, mark it "🔴 Urgent" and nearby fundis get an SMS priority alert. In Nairobi, most jobs receive 3–7 applications within 2 hours.
